\section{Additional results}

\paragraph{Update rule distributions}
Figures \ref{fig:emp_ur_dist}, \ref{fig:prg_ur_dist} and \ref{fig:rnd_ur_dist} show how homogeneous networks tend to overwhelmingly prefer the REP update rule against the other two.
Barabasi-Albert networks however show more variability and allow also the UI update rule to be competitive, while still favoring the REP one.
